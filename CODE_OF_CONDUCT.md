# Code of Conduct

Agent Runtime contributors are expected to keep project discussion respectful,
technical, and evidence-oriented.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and specific when reviewing issues, pull requests, or design proposals.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.
- Keep security-sensitive details out of public threads until a fix is available.
- Assume good intent, but prioritize user safety and runtime security boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing secrets, credentials, customer data, or exploit details in public project spaces.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers have redirected the thread.

## Reporting

For general conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the repository's
private maintainer channel when available.

For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of opening a
public issue with exploit details.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or take
other moderation actions when needed to protect the project and its users.
